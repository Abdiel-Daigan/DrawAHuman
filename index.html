Home

This website teaches you how to draw parts of the body. It provides step-by-step guides on drawing the head, hands, forearm, torso, and more. With my background in drawing, I offer reliable guides for anyone learning how to draw the human body.

Lessons

I.Fundamental

Shows the fundamentals that must be learned first before anything else.

II. Head and Face

Shows step-by-step guides on how to draw the head then how to draw the face.

III. Torso and Limbs

Teaches how to draw the torso and limbs by using basic fundamental shapes.

IV.Full-body and Gesture

To wrap things up, this is where they learn to apply the previous guides. Here is where I will also make a guide on doing gesture drawings for full body.
